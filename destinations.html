Explore Lakshadweep

Discover the breathtaking destinations of Lakshadweep.




Our Destinations

Bangaram Island

Bangaram Island is a serene paradise with its turquoise blue waters and white sandy beaches. It is perfect for those looking for tranquility and a chance to indulge in water sports like snorkeling and scuba diving.

Kavaratti Island

Kavaratti is the capital of Lakshadweep and known for its stunning lagoons and beautiful coral reefs. It offers a perfect blend of scenic beauty and cultural experiences.

Minicoy Island

Minicoy Island is known for its unique culture and the famous lighthouse. It is one of the most beautiful islands with lush green coconut palms and serene beaches.

Amini Island

Amini Island is known for its artistic stone crafts and coir products. It offers a rich cultural experience along with beautiful beaches and crystal-clear waters.

Agatti Island

Agatti Island is famous for its coral reefs and water sports like snorkeling. The island is ideal for nature lovers seeking peace and tranquility amidst natural beauty.

Kadmat Island

Kadmat Island is known for its pristine beaches and abundant marine life. It offers opportunities for water sports and relaxation in a tranquil setting.

Andrott Island

Andrott Island is known for its ancient Buddhist archaeological sites and beautiful beaches. It provides a glimpse into the historical and natural heritage of Lakshadweep.

Kalpeni Island

Kalpeni Island is known for its picturesque lagoons and vibrant coral reefs. It offers opportunities for snorkeling, kayaking, and relaxation in a tropical paradise.
